Indsoft is faithful in protecting the privacy and security of its customers and users of this website. The following points are the manner in which Indsoft will use, maintain and disclose the collected information from its clients and users of its website.

Loyalty towards your privacy

To allow us to provide you with a reasonable service, we collect a minimum amount of information about you. The intention of this Privacy Policy is to enable you to understand which personal identifying information of yours is collected, how and when we use your information, who has the right to use to this information, and how you can rectify any inaccuracies in the information.

Collection of information

We may gather any or all of the information when you visit our website. Such information will be collected via automated means like communications profiles and cookies. Personal information you give us depends on the type of service, support or sale inquiry and may include your name, address, telephone number, fax number and email address, dates of service provided, types of service provided, payment history, manner of payment, amount of payments, date of payments, domain name, credit card or other payment information. All the sensitive information is collected on a secure server and data is transferred. Any financial information we collect is used only to bill you for the products and services you purchased. If you purchase through credit card, this information will be forwarded to your credit card provider.

Keeping a record of files

Indsoft will use IP addresses for the purpose of analyzing its usage and trend pattern, track access and collect broad demographic information for aggregate use. When you visit our website we may collect information such as your IP address, type of browser or computer you use, the date and time of your visit, the pages you viewed, the name of our internet service provider, the number of links you click within our website, the location from which you accessed our website and the web page from which you linked to our website.

Cookies

Indsoft's website uses cookies (i.e. in-built facility for storing small text files that holds information on your computer's hard drive) to save your preferences and login information, and provide personalized functionality. You can reject cookies by changing your browser settings, but be aware that this will disable some of the functionality on Indsoft's website.

Updating and correcting personal information

If a client's personally identifiable information changes, or if a client no longer desires our services, we will provide a way to correct, update or remove that client's personal data in our records. Update to client contact details could be made via client portal, alternatively email could also be send to billing at indsoft dot net.

Modifications made to the Privacy Policy

Any changes to our Privacy Policy will be placed here and will replace this version of our Policy. Indsoft hereby reserves the right to amend, alter, modify, replace or suspend this Privacy Policy, from time to time, in its sole discretion. We will take reasonable steps to draw your attention to any changes in our Policy and will be effective as soon as we post on Indsoft website.

Usefulness of the information

The information that we get from our website is used for billing and to provide service and support to our clients, to review and determine our clients needs. We may also use the information we collect to seek your feedback, notify you about other products and services we think you will find valuable and about special offers. You may also notify us to discontinue of receiving such notifications. All safety measures are taken to avoid unauthorized access to this information.

Revealing of information

Indsoft will not reveal any personal information obtained about you from this website to third parties. We may use your personal information to keep in contact with you and inform you of developments associated with our business. We may reveal aggregate, anonymous data based on information collected from users to the potential partners, our affiliates, and reputable third parties. It is our responsibility to make sure that the said affiliates and service providers are fair and provide similar privacy protection to their clients and the community.

Avoiding and making changes to the system notifications

Clients will receive voluntary system updates, like newsletters, invoices, and mailings by default. Any client who voluntarily receives these updates may unsubscribe at any time. Clients may not opt out of possibly receiving important system notifications, in the rare event that such notification would be necessary.

Protection and safety measures

Indsoft will make an attempt to follow industry standard security measures to prevent the loss, misuse, alteration or unauthorized disclosure of information under our control. Indsoft uses safety measures including and not limited to: physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online. When we ask clients or users to provide financial information (such as a credit card number) that data is protected using Secure Sockets Layer ("SSL") technology.
